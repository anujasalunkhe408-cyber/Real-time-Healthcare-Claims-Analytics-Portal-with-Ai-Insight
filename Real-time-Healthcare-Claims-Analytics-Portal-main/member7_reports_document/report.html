The Reports & Documentation module is responsible for collecting claim information from the healthcare database and generating daily, weekly and monthly reports. It also generates AI Insight Reports, Fraud Detection Reports, Prediction Reports, Trend Analysis, Audit Logs and Exportable PDF Documentation. The objective of this module is to provide accurate, real-time healthcare claim analytics for administrators and analysts.
